Oggy Wiki - Characters

A wiki-style page about the main characters in Oggy and the Cockroaches.

Contents

Oggy Jack Joey Dee Dee Marky Olivia Bob

Oggy

Oggy
Species Cat
Color Blue
Role Main character
Personality Calm, lazy, friendly
Enemies Cockroaches
Friends Jack, Olivia

Oggy is the main character of Oggy and the Cockroaches. He enjoys relaxing, eating snacks, and watching TV.

However, his life is constantly disturbed by three cockroaches who prank him every day.

Jack

Jack
Species Cat
Color Green
Role Oggy's cousin
Personality Aggressive, brave, strong
Enemies Cockroaches, Bob
Friends Oggy

Jack is Oggy's cousin. He is strong and aggressive, and he always tries to fight the cockroaches.

Even though Jack is helpful, he often causes chaos and damage while trying to solve problems.

Joey

Joey
Species Cockroach
Color Purple
Role Leader of cockroaches
Personality Smart, evil, confident
Enemies Oggy, Jack
Friends Dee Dee, Marky

Joey is the leader of the cockroaches. He is the smartest and most confident cockroach.

He enjoys bullying Oggy and is usually the one who creates the plans for pranks.

Dee Dee

Dee Dee
Species Cockroach
Color Green
Role Cockroach member
Personality Hungry, silly, greedy
Enemies Oggy, Jack
Friends Joey, Marky

Dee Dee is the hungriest cockroach. He is obsessed with food and will eat almost anything.

He is not very intelligent, but he is funny and causes a lot of trouble.

Marky

Marky
Species Cockroach
Color Brown
Role Cockroach member
Personality Lazy, calm, chill
Enemies Oggy, Jack
Friends Joey, Dee Dee

Marky is the tallest cockroach. He is calm and relaxed compared to the others.

Even though he acts lazy, he still joins the group when they prank Oggy.

Olivia

Olivia
Species Cat
Color White / Pink
Role Oggy's girlfriend
Personality Kind, sweet, helpful
Enemies Cockroaches
Friends Oggy

Olivia is Oggy's girlfriend. She is kind and caring, and she often supports Oggy.

Olivia is one of the few characters who brings calmness into Oggy's life.

Bob

Bob
Species Bulldog
Color Brown
Role Enemy character
Personality Angry, aggressive, strong
Enemies Jack, Oggy
Friends Sometimes none

Bob is a bulldog who often appears as a strong enemy. He is aggressive and grumpy.

Bob usually fights Jack and scares Oggy, creating even more chaos.
